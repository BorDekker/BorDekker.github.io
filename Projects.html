Project No Noise

Bon Appmobile

A Unreal Engine 5 VR project where you're driving around the world to make and deliver pizza. You'll make pizza in your vehicle, you can use a sauce gun to add sauce and use the ingredients to add toppings or use them to make the vehicle go faster or slower.

Engine: Unreal Engine 5.2

Programming language: Blueprints

Experiment Oneiro

A Unreal Engine 5 VR project where you are trapped in a room and must solve 3 puzzles to escape within the alotted time.

Engine: Unreal Engine 5.3

Programming language: Niagara stack simulations
