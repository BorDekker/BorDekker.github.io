About Me

I'm Bor Dekker, an 19-year-old Unreal Engine 5 game developer from the Netherlands.
I specialize in Unreal Engine 5, Blueprints and C++. I would like to have experience working with MetaHuman & more experience working with Houdini in Unreal Engine 5.

I would love it if I can create games like Bloodborne, Layers of Fear, Lies of P and Black Myth Wukong in Unreal Engine 5. One of my dream projects is making a horror soulslike in Unreal Engine 5, combining my favourite genres into 1 game.

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, reading books / manga, watching movies / anime and watching devlogs of people creating their own game(s). Some of my favorite video games are Bloodborne, Elden Ring, God of War, Cuphead and Layers of Fear.

I'm looking for an internship at a company that will help me develop my skills in Unreal Engine 5, Blueprints, C++ and give me some experience working with MetaHuman.
