About Me

I'm Bor Dekker, an 19-year-old Unreal Engine 5 game developer from the Netherlands.
I specialize in Unreal Engine 5, Blueprints and C++. I would like to have some experience in working with MetaHuman.

Games like, Bloodborne, God Of War, Elden Ring, Castlevania, Cuphead have inspired me to create video games.

My heroes in the game industry include game directors Cory Barlog, Hidetaka Miyazaki, Maja Moldenhauer and the game company Konami.

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, reading books / manga, watching movies / anime, watching devlogs on YouTube and programming. My top 5 favorite video games are Bloodborne, Elden Ring, God of War, Cuphead and Castlevania Symphony of the Night.
